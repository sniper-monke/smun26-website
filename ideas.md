# SMUN 26 Website Design Brainstorm

## Selected Design Approach: **Diplomatic Elegance with Modern Minimalism**

### Design Movement
**Neo-Classical Diplomacy** — A fusion of traditional diplomatic aesthetics (gold, navy, classical geometry) with contemporary minimalist design. Inspired by official government websites, UN headquarters design, and premium conference branding.

### Core Principles
1. **Prestige Through Restraint** — Every element serves a purpose; no decoration without function. Gold accents are strategic, not excessive.
2. **Geometric Precision** — Clean lines, perfect symmetry, and mathematical proportions create authority and trust.
3. **Dark Sophistication** — Deep navy background conveys diplomacy and formality; gold provides warmth and prestige.
4. **Progressive Disclosure** — Information reveals itself through smooth animations and transitions, respecting user attention.

### Color Philosophy
- **Primary: Deep Navy Blue** (`#0F1B2E` or similar) — Represents diplomacy, authority, and international governance. Creates a formal, trustworthy foundation.
- **Secondary: Metallic Gold** (`#D4AF37` or similar) — Accents, borders, highlights. Represents prestige, achievement, and excellence.
- **Tertiary: Off-white/Cream** (`#F5F1E8`) — Text, subtle backgrounds. Provides contrast without harshness; feels refined.
- **Accent: Soft Blue Glow** — Subtle luminescence around key elements (cards, buttons) to add depth without breaking formality.

**Emotional Intent:** Confidence, authority, elegance, global perspective, diplomatic professionalism.

### Layout Paradigm
**Asymmetric Elegance with Centered Focal Points**
- Hero sections use full-width imagery with offset text placement
- Committees and content sections alternate between left-aligned and right-aligned layouts
- Navigation is minimal and top-aligned; doesn't compete with content
- Whitespace is generous—breathing room between sections
- Avoid centered grids; instead use natural flow with strategic alignment

### Signature Elements
1. **Gold Accent Lines** — Thin horizontal lines (1-2px) separate sections, frame headings, and underline interactive elements on hover
2. **Compass Rose Motif** — Subtle compass patterns appear in section dividers and as watermarks; ties back to the logo's compass
3. **Glassmorphism Cards** — Semi-transparent cards with subtle backdrop blur, gold borders, and soft shadows for committee listings and key information

### Interaction Philosophy
- **Hover States:** Gold underlines expand, cards lift with soft shadow growth, text gains subtle glow
- **Transitions:** All animations are smooth (300-500ms), never jarring
- **Micro-interactions:** Buttons respond immediately; loading states are elegant (spinning compass or animated particles)
- **Feedback:** Every click/interaction provides visual confirmation through color shift or animation

### Animation Guidelines
- **Entrance Animations:** Fade-in with slight upward movement (100-200ms) for sections as user scrolls
- **Cinematic Intro:** 2-3 second sequence with faint stars, gold lines animating outward, logo fading in with glow
- **Hover Animations:** Gold underlines expand, cards lift, text gains subtle glow (all 300ms)
- **Scroll Animations:** Parallax effects on hero sections; subtle fade-in for text blocks
- **Globe Animation:** Slow rotation (30-40s full rotation), user can drag to interact; smooth lighting with gold highlights on landmasses

### Typography System
- **Headings:** Serif font (e.g., Playfair Display, Cormorant Garamond) — Bold, elegant, commanding presence
  - H1: 3.5rem, font-weight 700, letter-spacing +0.05em
  - H2: 2.5rem, font-weight 600, letter-spacing +0.03em
  - H3: 1.75rem, font-weight 600
- **Body Text:** Modern sans-serif (e.g., Inter, Lato) — Clean, readable, professional
  - Body: 1rem, line-height 1.6, font-weight 400
  - Accent text: 0.875rem, font-weight 500, letter-spacing +0.02em
- **Hierarchy:** Serif for importance, sans-serif for clarity. Never mix more than two font families.

✅ Dark color palette (deep navy background)
✅ Gold metallic accents throughout
✅ Serif headings + sans-serif body
✅ Glassmorphism cards with gold borders
✅ Asymmetric layouts with generous whitespace
✅ Smooth, premium animations (300-500ms)
✅ Compass rose and geometric motifs
✅ SMUN logo as centerpiece of branding
